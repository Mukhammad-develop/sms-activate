# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our SMS-Activate Telegram Bot (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Telegram User ID**: Your unique Telegram identifier (numeric)
- **Telegram Username**: Your public Telegram username (if available)
- **Name**: Your Telegram display name/first name
- **Language Preference**: Language you select for the bot interface

### 2.2 Transaction Information
- **Purchase History**: Services purchased, phone numbers received, order IDs
- **Balance Information**: Account balance and transaction records
- **Payment Data**: Transaction amounts, timestamps, descriptions
- **Order Details**: Activation IDs, phone numbers, service codes, countries

### 2.3 Technical Information
- **Messages**: Commands and interactions with the bot
- **IP Address**: May be collected through Telegram's API
- **Usage Data**: Frequency of service usage, features accessed

### 2.4 SMS Content
- **SMS Codes**: Verification codes received through our service
- **SMS Text**: Full text of SMS messages received
- **Phone Numbers**: Temporary virtual phone numbers assigned

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Process and fulfill your service requests
- Provide virtual phone numbers for verification
- Deliver SMS messages to you
- Manage your account balance and transactions
- Send order confirmations and notifications

### 3.2 Account Management
- Create and maintain your user account
- Authenticate your identity
- Process refunds and cancellations
- Maintain transaction history

### 3.3 Communication
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send service updates and notifications
- Deliver order status updates

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and court orders
- Protect our rights and prevent fraud

### 3.5 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Improve service functionality
- Troubleshoot technical issues

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Data is stored on secure servers
- Telegram user data is stored in compliance with Telegram's policies
- Transaction data is encrypted at rest

### 4.2 Security Measures
- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data retained for 2 years after last activity
- **Transaction Records**: Retained for 7 years for legal/compliance purposes
- **SMS Content**: Deleted after 30 days or upon user request

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 SMS-Activate API
- We use SMS-Activate API to provide virtual phone numbers
- Your requests are transmitted to SMS-Activate
- SMS-Activate's privacy policy applies to their service
- We do not control SMS-Activate's data practices

### 5.2 Telegram
- Service operates through Telegram platform
- Telegram's privacy policy applies: https://telegram.org/privacy
- We receive user data through Telegram Bot API
- We do not store your Telegram password or login credentials

### 5.3 Payment Processors
- Payment processing handled by administrator (manual)
- No third-party payment processors used
- All payment communications are private and secure

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 6.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
- We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
- Your data is not shared with advertisers or marketers

### 6.2 Limited Sharing
We may share information only in these circumstances:
- **With SMS-Activate**: Necessary to fulfill service requests
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or court order
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale
- **With Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

### 6.3 Anonymized Data
- We may use anonymized, aggregated data for analytics
- This data cannot identify individual users

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access Your Data
- View your account balance via `/balance`
- Review transaction history via `/history`
- Check your orders via `/myorders`

### 7.2 Data Deletion
- Request account deletion by contacting administrator
- All personal data will be deleted within 30 days

### 7.3 Data Correction
- Update your language preference via `/language`
- Contact administrator to correct account information

### 7.4 Opt-Out

- Disable notifications through Telegram settings
